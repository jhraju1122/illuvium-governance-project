Proposal Types

Understanding Governance Proposals

In Illuvium's governance structure, four types of proposals guide our collective decision-making process.
Each proposal serves a unique purpose within the ecosystem.

IIP

Illuvium Improvement
Proposal

These proposals focus on broad, project-wide developments and improvements, from protocol updates to feature implementations.

ICCP

Illuvium Configuration Change Proposal

Tailored for more granular updates, ICCPs address specific changes in the game's configuration or operational adjustments.

IGPP

Illuvium Game Pitch Proposal

Game Pitch Proposals are structured pathways for the inception, evaluation, and development of game proposals. It is aimed at harnessing the collective intelligence and creative power of the Illuvium community.

IPTF

Illuvium Tournament Fund Proposal

Dedicated to the Illuvium PvP Tournament Fund, IPTF proposals manage the allocation of funds for player-organized tournaments, fostering a competitive community.
